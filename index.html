About Glam On Academy

Built on trust,
driven by craft.

We opened Glam On Academy because we believed White Rock deserved a salon that puts genuine skill and client experience above everything else. No upselling. No rushed appointments. Just great hair.

Our team of talented stylists each bring a unique specialty — from precision cuts to editorial colour — and collaborate constantly to keep each other sharp.
